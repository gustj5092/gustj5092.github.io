I am an undergraduate researcher in Mechanical Engineering at **Yonsei University**, working in the [Machine Learning and Control Systems (MLCS) Lab](https://mlcs.yonsei.ac.kr).

My research focuses on **safety-critical control** for autonomous systems — particularly Robust Control Barrier Functions (CBF), MPC/MPPI-based planning, and End-to-End (E2E) autonomous racing. I am a co-author of an IEEE L-CSS + CDC submission on uncertainty-aware CBF design with analytical QCQP solutions.

Beyond research, I lead **YAX**, a robotics and autonomous driving club at Yonsei, and have competed in multiple national autonomous driving competitions including HL FMA, KSAE Formula, and F1Tenth Korea Championship.

I believe that **without challenge, there is no opportunity** — and I enjoy turning competition setbacks into deeper technical insights.

**Research Interests:**
- Safety-Critical Control (Robust CBF, MPPI Stability)
- End-to-End Autonomous Racing (Game-Theoretic CBF + E2E)
- Multi-Agent Planning & Trajectory Verification
- Perception (LiDAR-Camera Fusion, SLAM)
