Hyeonseo Oh

B.S. Mechanical Engineering · Yonsei University · MLCS Lab Intern

Seoul, Republic of Korea

gustj5092@yonsei.ac.kr

I am an undergraduate researcher in Mechanical Engineering at Yonsei University, working in the Machine Learning and Control Systems (MLCS) Lab.

My research focuses on safety-critical control for autonomous systems — Robust Control Barrier Functions (CBF), MPC/MPPI-based planning, and End-to-End autonomous racing. Co-author of an IEEE L-CSS + CDC submission on uncertainty-aware CBF design.

Research Interests: Safety-Critical Control · Robust CBF · E2E Autonomous Racing · LiDAR-Camera Fusion
